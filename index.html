Academic projects

major project




As part of our college major project, our team collaborated on 'Predicting Student Performance in an Educational Game Using a Hidden Markov Model.' This project involved developing and implementing a Hidden Markov Model (HMM) to analyze and forecast student performance within an educational gaming environment. By integrating complex statistical methods with educational data, our team aimed to deepen the understanding of student learning behaviors and provide educators with predictive insights into student progress.

minor project




For our final year project, we created a cutting-edge Traffic Rules Violation Detection System. Our team harnessed the power of advanced image processing techniques and machine learning algorithms to automatically identify and report traffic rule violations, including speeding, signal violations, and unauthorized lane changes. By combining real-time video surveillance with an intelligent detection system, we aimed to significantly improve road safety and ensure stricter adherence to traffic laws. This project highlights our collective expertise in computer vision, data analysis, and software engineering, demonstrating our ability to address practical challenges through innovative technology.
